Why Not You?

No one asked what my dream is.

No one asked what path I am walking on.

They only ask one thing:
"Why not you?"

Two people can work equally hard.
Two people can dream differently.
One may reach early,
The other may be walking a longer road.

But society measures both with the same scale,
And that hurts.

When one success happens,
A wave flows.

A wave of comparison.
A wave of words that sound like advice,
But feel like judgment.

Suddenly, you are no longer a struggler.
You are labelled a failure.
